Next Level Student Summit

The Next Level Student Summit is a transformative student gathering focused on life beyond the classroom. It exposes students to conversations around purpose, growth, career clarity, and building a meaningful future while still in school. The summit is designed to challenge mindsets, broaden perspectives, and equip students to thrive beyond academics.

It will feature experienced professionals and mentors who will provide practical guidance on personal development, career positioning, and financial literacy.

Event Details:
Date: Saturday,March 29, 2026
Time: 10:00 AM
Venue: Community Hall, University of Calabar
