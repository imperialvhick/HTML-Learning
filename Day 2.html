Imperial Chocolate Chip

Here is the best guide to creating tiny chocolate chip cookies!
You can satisfy your sweet taste without going overboard with these bite-sized delights. Make delicious, chewy-on-the-inside, crispy-on-the-outside little chocolate chip cookies that everyone will adore by following this easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacao Baking Chips
  • 1 cup Walnuts or pecans, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375ÂºF.
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In large mixing bowl, beat butter with sugar, and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixture. Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!
