Challenge Rules
  • The challenge runs from July 12, 2026 to October 20, 2026. Only sales made within this window count.
  • Everyone who hits a tier wins. This is not a competition. If 500 affiliates hit 30 sales, all 500 get rewarded.
  • If you exceed a tier, you are rewarded for every tier you cross. For example, 320 sales earns you the 300-sales prize.
  • Only verified, completed sales through your affiliate link count toward your total.
  • For Cash OR Gadget prizes — you choose which you prefer at the time of claiming your reward.
  • Prizes are disbursed after the challenge ends and sales have been verified. You will be contacted via the details on your affiliate account.
Important: This challenge is one prize per affiliate account. Make sure all your sales are tracked through your registered affiliate link for them to count.
